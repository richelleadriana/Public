### Post 2 - 6d Edited

Planning SEO content for the 2nd half of 2026?

PLEASE do this first:

1. Get all your blog URLs into a spreadsheet.

Use Screaming Frog to crawl your site and export the URLs. Then enrich the sheet with data from:

- Ahrefs
- Google Analytics
- Google Search Console

2. Find the keywords each important URL ranks for.

Use Google Search Console or Ahrefs, then add:

- Primary keyword
- Secondary keywords / variations
- Current rankings for target keywords

If a page isn't ranking for anything, find the most relevant target keyword in Ahrefs and add that too.

3. Audit every post and assign an action:

- Leave: already reached its potential
- Merge: cannibalising or too similar to another page
- Delete: no longer useful or valuable
- Update: small improvements needed
- Rewrite: major improvements needed

4. Prioritise updates and rewrites where:

- target keywords sit in positions 4-20
- the page has higher conversion or revenue potential
- there are obvious improvement opportunities
- the page isn't yet being retrieved by LLMs

Reminder:

Not all of your Q3 content plan needs to be new.

You're probably sitting on many quick wins in content that you've already published.
